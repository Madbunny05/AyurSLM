### AYURVEDIC PROTOCOLS: ELDERLY CARE & CHRONIC DISEASES

## CATEGORY: ELDERLY CARE (JARA CHIKITSA / RASAYANA)

# Principle of Aging
- Aging is governed by Vata Dosha, leading to dryness, degeneration, and weakened Agni.
- Goal: Maintain quality of life, mobility, and cognitive clarity.

# Condition: Joint Pain and Stiffness (Sandhigata Vata)
- Symptoms: Cracking sounds in joints, pain on movement, morning stiffness.
- Herbs: Guggulu, Shallaki (Boswellia), Mahanarayan Taila (for external application).
- Therapy: Janu Basti (oil pooling for knees), Kati Basti (for back pain).

# Condition: Cognitive Health & Memory (Smrutti)
- Symptoms: Forgetfulness, confusion, mental fatigue.
- Rejuvenation: Medhya Rasayanas like Brahmi, Shankhapushpi, and Bacopa.
- Daily Ritual: Nasya (medicated oil in nostrils) and Shirodhara (oil pouring on forehead).

# Condition: Digestive Weakness in Seniors
- Approach: Light, warm, and easily digestible (Laghu) meals.
- Support: Triphala Churna for bowel regularity; Ginger/Ajwain for gas and bloating.

## CATEGORY: CHRONIC & METABOLIC DISORDERS

# Condition: Heart Health (Hridroga)
- Focus: Managing cholesterol, blood flow, and stress.
- Herbs: Arjuna (bark decoction), Ashwagandha (stress), Garlic (cholesterol).
- Routine: Half-an-hour of gentle walking and Pranayama (Nadi Shodhana).

# Condition: Hypertension (Rakta Rakshachapa)
- Ayurvedic View: Imbalance of Vata and Pitta in the blood channels.
- Support: Sarpagandha (under supervision), Brahmi, Punarnava.
- Dietary: Low salt, cooling foods, and moon-breathing (Chandra Bhedana).

# Condition: Kidney & Urinary Health (Mutravaha Srotas)
- Condition: Kidney Stones (Ashmari).
- Herbs: Pashanbhed, Gokshura, Varuna.
- Support: Drink plenty of water and avoid oxalate-rich foods like spinach.

# Condition: Cancer Supportive Care (Arbud)
- Role: Boosting immunity during/after chemotherapy and managing fatigue.
- Herbs: Turmeric (Curcumin), Guduchi, Ashwagandha.
- Focus: Sattvic diet and mental grounding.
