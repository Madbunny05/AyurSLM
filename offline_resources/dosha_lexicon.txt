### DOSHA LEXICON: SYMPTOMS & IMBALANCES

#### 1. VATA DOSHA (Air & Space)
- **Balanced State**: Creativity, enthusiasm, lightness of body, alert mind.
- **Vata-Vriddhi (Imbalance - Increase)**:
  - Physical: Dry skin, joint cracking, constipation, insomnia, tremors, cold sensitivity, weight loss.
  - Mental: Anxiety, fear, racing thoughts, restlessness, forgetfulness.
- **Vata-Kshaya (Imbalance - Decrease)**: 
  - Lethargy, loss of speech, mental confusion, weak digestion.

#### 2. PITTA DOSHA (Fire & Water)
- **Balanced State**: Strong digestion, intelligence, leadership, warm body temperature.
- **Pitta-Vriddhi (Imbalance - Increase)**:
  - Physical: Acid reflux, skin rashes, inflammation, excessive sweating, early graying, red eyes.
  - Mental: Anger, irritability, impatience, perfectionism, jealousy.
- **Pitta-Kshaya (Imbalance - Decrease)**: 
  - Coldness, dull skin, loss of appetite, indigestion.

#### 3. KAPHA DOSHA (Earth & Water)
- **Balanced State**: Stamina, calmness, strong joints, soft skin, deep sleep.
- **Kapha-Vriddhi (Imbalance - Increase)**:
  - Physical: Weight gain, congestion, excessive sleep, sluggishness, slow digestion, oily skin.
  - Mental: Attachment, greed, stubbornness, depression, lack of motivation.
- **Kapha-Kshaya (Imbalance - Decrease)**: 
  - Dryness of joints, palpitations, dizziness, loss of sleep.
