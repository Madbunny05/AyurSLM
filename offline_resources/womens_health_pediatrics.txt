### AYURVEDIC PROTOCOLS: WOMEN'S HEALTH & PEDIATRICS

## CATEGORY: WOMEN'S HEALTH (STREE ROGA)

# Condition: Menstrual Irregularities (Artava Vyapad)
- Vata Type: Short, irregular, scanty, and painful cycles.
- Pitta Type: Excessive flow, burning sensation, irritability.
- Kapha Type: Heavy, long cycles with clots and lethargy.
- Important Herbs: Ashokarishta (for uterine health), Shatavari (hormonal balance), Lodhra (excessive bleeding).
- Treatment: Dosha-specific diet, Abhyanga (oil massage) during the month, and Rasayana (rejuvenation) therapies.

# Condition: PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)
- Symptoms: Irregular periods, weight gain, acne, facial hair.
- Ayurvedic View: Linked to Kapha and Vata imbalance in Shukra Dhatu.
- Recommended Herbs: Chandraprabha Vati, Kanchanar Guggulu, Fenugreek (Methi), Cinnamon.
- Lifestyle: Low Kapha diet (avoid dairy/sweets), regular intense exercise, and stress management.

# Condition: Menopause (Rajonivrutti)
- Symptoms: Hot flashes, mood swings, dryness, bone density loss.
- Ayurvedic View: Natural transition to Vata stage of life.
- Herbs: Brahmi, Gotukola, Shatavari (phytoestrogens).
- Routine: Warm oil massages, grounding yoga poses, and Tarpaka (nourishing) foods.

# Condition: Postnatal Care (Sutika Paricharya)
- Focus: Restoring the mother's energy and supporting lactation.
- Diet: Warm, mushy, easily digestible foods with ginger, cumin, and fennel.
- Herbs: Dashamoola (inflammation/pain), Vidarikand (lactation), Ashwagandha (strength).
- Important Rituals: Daily Abhyanga with warm oils for 40 days to ground Vata.

## CATEGORY: PEDIATRIC CARE (KAUMARABHRITYA)

# Focus: Building Immunity (Vyadhikshamatva)
- Childhood is the Kapha stage of life; children are prone to mucus and respiratory issues.
- Ritual: Swarnaprashana (Gold ash with honey/ghee) to boost intelligence and immunity.
- Immune Herbs: Guduchi, Amla, Tulsi, Golden Milk (Turmeric milk).

# Condition: Common Childhood Ailments
- Cough/Cold: Tulsi tea, Ginger and Honey drops, Steam with Eucalyptus.
- Colic/Gas: Apply Hing (Asafoetida) paste around the navel; warm fennel water.
- Diarrhea: Pomegranate peel decoction; Rice water with a pinch of salt/ginger.

# Condition: Behavioral & Developmental Support
- For Hyperactivity/Anxiety: Herbs like Brahmi and Shankhapushpi to calm the mind.
- Diet for Kids: Fresh, seasonal foods, Ghee (for brain development), and avoiding processed sugar.
