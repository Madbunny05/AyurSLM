### AYURVEDIC HERBS & REMEDIES ENCYCLOPEDIA

#### ASHWAGANDHA (Withania somnifera)
- **Properties**: Rasa: Tikta, Kashaya; Virya: Ushna; Vipaka: Madhura.
- **Effects**: Balances Vata and Kapha; may increase Pitta if taken in excess.
- **Benefits**: Stress relief, immunity, strength, nervous system support.
- **Preparation**: Mix 1 tsp of powder in warm milk with honey at night.

#### TULSI (Holy Basil)
- **Properties**: Rasa: Katu, Tikta; Virya: Ushna; Vipaka: Katu.
- **Effects**: Balances Kapha and Vata; increases Pitta.
- **Benefits**: Respiratory health, fever, cough, digestive support, mental clarity.
- **Preparation**: Fresh leaf decoction (tea) or dried leaf powder.

#### TURMERIC (Haridra)
- **Properties**: Rasa: Tikta, Katu; Virya: Ushna; Vipaka: Katu.
- **Effects**: Balances all three doshas (Tridoshic).
- **Benefits**: Anti-inflammatory, skin health, blood purifier, wound healing.
- **Preparation**: Golden milk (milk + turmeric + pinch of black pepper).

#### BRAHMI (Bacopa monnieri)
- **Properties**: Rasa: Tikta, Kashaya; Virya: Sheeta; Vipaka: Madhura.
- **Effects**: Balances Pitta and Kapha; good for Vata in moderation.
- **Benefits**: Brain tonic, memory enhancer, nervous system balancer.
- **Preparation**: Brahmi Ghrita (ghee) or powder with warm water.

#### TRIPHALA (Three Fruits)
- **Properties**: Tridoshic (Amla, Haritaki, Bibhitaki).
- **Effects**: Balances Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
- **Benefits**: Digestive cleanse, rejuvenation, eye health.
- **Preparation**: 1 tsp powder in warm water before sleep.
