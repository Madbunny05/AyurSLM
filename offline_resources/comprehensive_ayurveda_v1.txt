### COMPREHENSIVE AYURVEDIC KNOWLEDGE BASE - V1

## CATEGORY: DIGESTIVE DISORDERS (ANNAVAHA SROTAS)

# Condition: Gastritis / Acidity (Amla Pitta)
- Symptoms: Burning sensation in chest and throat, sour belching, indigestion, nausea, headache.
- Ayurvedic View: Imbalance of Pitta Dosha and Agni (digestive fire).
- Herbs: Amla (Indian Gooseberry), Shatavari, Licorice (Yashthimadhu), Aloe Vera.
- Remedy: Mix 1 tsp Amla powder with 1/2 tsp honey and consume twice daily after meals. Avoid spicy and fried foods.

# Condition: Constipation (Vibandha)
- Symptoms: Hard stools, infrequent bowel movements, abdominal bloating, feeling of incomplete evacuation.
- Ayurvedic View: Imbalance of Vata Dosha, specifically Apana Vayu.
- Herbs: Haritaki, Triphala, Senna (limited use), Castor Oil.
- Remedy: Take 1 tsp Triphala Churna with warm water before bedtime. Increase intake of ghee and fibrous vegetables.

# Condition: Bloating and Gas (Adhmana)
- Symptoms: Abdominal distension, flatulence, rumbling sounds in the gut, discomfort after eating.
- Ayurvedic View: Accumulation of Vayu (Air) in the digestive tract.
- Herbs: Hing (Asafoetida), Ajwain (Carom Seeds), Ginger, Fennel.
- Remedy: Drink a glass of warm water with a pinch of Hing and Ajwain 15 minutes after meals.

# Condition: IBS / Diarrhea (Atisara / Grahani)
- Symptoms: Frequent loose motions, mucus in stool, abdominal cramps, weak appetite.
- Ayurvedic View: Weak Agni and Pitta/Vata imbalance.
- Herbs: Kutaj (Holarrhena), Bilva (Bael Fruit), Pomegranate peel.
- Remedy: Take 2 tbsp of Bael fruit pulp mixed with buttermilk twice daily.

## CATEGORY: RESPIRATORY DISORDERS (PRANAVAHA SROTAS)

# Condition: Common Cold (Pratishyaya)
- Symptoms: Nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, mild fever, throat irritation.
- Ayurvedic View: Increase in Kapha and Vata Doshas.
- Herbs: Tulsi (Holy Basil), Ginger, Turmeric, Black Pepper (Trikatu).
- Remedy: Boil 5 leaves of Tulsi and 1 inch of crushed Ginger in 2 cups of water. Reduce to 1 cup, add honey, and drink warm.

# Condition: Cough (Kasa)
- Symptoms: Dry or wet cough, chest congestion, sore throat.
- Ayurvedic View: Kapha imbalance (wet) or Vata imbalance (dry).
- Herbs: Vasaka (Adhatoda), Sitopaladi Churna, Honey, Licorice.
- Remedy: Mix 1/2 tsp Sitopaladi Churna with 1 tsp honey and lick slowly 3 times a day.

# Condition: Asthma / Shortness of Breath (Shwasa)
- Symptoms: Difficulty breathing, wheezing, chest tightness.
- Ayurvedic View: Blockage of Prana Vayu by Kapha.
- Herbs: Bharangi, Pushkarmool, Ginger, Garlic.
- Remedy: Drink warm water mixed with 1 tsp ginger juice and a pinch of rock salt (Saindhava Namak) during mild episodes.

## CATEGORY: MENTAL HEALTH AND SLEEP (MANASA ROGA)

# Condition: Stress and Anxiety (Chittodvega)
- Symptoms: Continuous worrying, restlessness, palpitations, racing thoughts.
- Ayurvedic View: Vata imbalance affecting the Majja Dhatu (nervous system).
- Herbs: Ashwagandha, Brahmi, Shankhapushpi, Jatamansi.
- Remedy: Take 1 tsp Ashwagandha powder with warm milk at night to calm the nervous system.

# Condition: Insomnia (Anidra)
- Symptoms: Difficulty falling asleep, frequent waking, unrefreshing sleep.
- Ayurvedic View: Vata and Pitta imbalance disrupting Tarpaka Kapha.
- Herbs: Tagar (Indian Valerian), Ashwagandha, Brahmi, Poppy seeds.
- Remedy: Massage the soles of the feet with warm Sesame oil (Pada Abhyanga) before sleep.

## CATEGORY: SKIN DISORDERS (KUSHTHA)

# Condition: Acne (Yuvana Pidaka)
- Symptoms: Pimples, blackheads, oily skin, inflammation on face/back.
- Ayurvedic View: Pitta and Rakta (blood) imbalance.
- Herbs: Neem, Manjistha, Turmeric, Sandalwood.
- Remedy: Apply a paste of Neem powder and Turmeric mixed with rose water on affected areas for 20 minutes daily.

# Condition: Eczema / Rashes (Vicarchika)
- Symptoms: Redness, itching, oozing or dry scales on skin.
- Ayurvedic View: Kapha and Pitta imbalance in the blood.
- Herbs: Khadir (Acacia catechu), Neem oil, Coconut oil with Camphor.
- Remedy: Apply pure coconut oil mixed with a pinch of Camphor to soothe the itching and dryness.

## CATEGORY: CHRONIC / METABOLIC CONDITIONS

# Condition: Diabetes (Madhumetha / Prameha)
- Symptoms: Frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue, slow healing of wounds.
- Ayurvedic View: Kapha dominance affecting Meda (fat) and Mutra (urine).
- Herbs: Karela (Bitter Gourd), Jamun (Java Plum), Turmeric, Amla (Nisha-Amalaki), Vijaysar.
- Remedy: Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric and 1/2 tsp Amla powder (Nisha-Amalaki) in warm water every morning on an empty stomach.

# Condition: Obesity (Sthaulya)
- Symptoms: Excess body weight, lethargy, breathlessness on exertion.
- Ayurvedic View: Excessive accumulation of Meda Dhatu (fat) and slow metabolism.
- Herbs: Guggulu, Triphala, Fenugreek (Methi), Honey (with warm water).
- Remedy: Drink a glass of warm water with 1 tsp honey and 1/2 lemon juice first thing in the morning.

# Condition: Joint Pain / Arthritis (Amavata / Sandhivata)
- Symptoms: Pain, swelling, stiffness in joints, especially in the morning.
- Ayurvedic View: Accumulation of Ama (toxins) and Vata imbalance.
- Herbs: Guggulu, Shallaki (Boswellia), Ginger, Nirgundi.
- Remedy: Apply warm sesame oil mixed with dry ginger powder to the joints. Consume warm foods and avoid cold drinks.

## CATEGORY: GENERAL WELLNESS

# Condition: Low Immunity (Ojas Kshaya)
- Symptoms: Frequent illness, fatigue, lack of mental clarity.
- Ayurvedic View: Depletion of Ojas (the essence of all tissues).
- Herbs: Guduchi (Giloy), Amla, Ashwagandha, Chyawanprash.
- Remedy: Consume 1 tsp of Chyawanprash with warm milk daily. Take 500mg of Guduchi extract daily.

# Condition: Hair Fall (Khalitya)
- Symptoms: Excessive hair loss, thinning of hair, dandruff.
- Ayurvedic View: Pitta imbalance at the roots of the hair (Asthi Dhatu).
- Herbs: Bhringraj, Brahmi, Amla, Hibiscus.
- Remedy: Massage the scalp with warm Bhringraj oil twice a week. Drink Amla juice daily.
