### AYURVEDIC PROTOCOLS: DEEP DIVE INTO RESPIRATORY, SKIN, & DIGESTIVE SYSTEMS

## SYSTEM: RESPIRATORY (PRANAVAHA SROTAS)

# Condition: Sinusitis (Pinasa / Peenasa)
- Symptoms: Facial pain, headache, nasal discharge, loss of smell.
- Herbs: Trikatu (Ginger, Pepper, Long Pepper), Tulsi, Neem (for steam).
- Ritual: Jalaneti (saline nasal irrigation) and Anu Taila Nasya.

# Condition: Chronic Bronchitis / Allergies
- Approach: Expelling excess Kapha and strengthening lung tissue.
- Herbs: Vasaka (Adhatoda), Kantakari, Pushkarmool.
- Diet: Avoid curd, cold drinks, and heavy sweets.

## SYSTEM: SKIN & BLOOD (RAKTAVAHA SROTAS)

# Condition: Psoriasis (Kitibha)
- Symptoms: Thick, red, scaly patches on skin, itching.
- Ayurvedic View: Deep-seated Pitta and Kapha imbalance in the blood.
- Herbs: Khadir (Acacia), Manjistha (Blood Purifier), Neem Oil.
- Important: Vamana (therapeutic vomiting) under medical supervision for Kapha-type patches.

# Condition: Vitiligo (Shvitra)
- Symptoms: White patches on skin due to loss of pigment.
- Herbs: Bakuchi (Psoralea), Turmeric, Manjistha.
- Support: Avoiding "Viruddha Ahara" (incompatible food combinations like milk and fish).

## SYSTEM: DIGESTIVE (ANNAVAHA SROTAS)

# Condition: Fatty Liver / Liver Support (Yakrit Roga)
- Herbs: Bhumyamalaki (Phyllanthus), Katuki, Kalmegh.
- Support: Avoid alcohol, oily fats, and heavy animal proteins.

# Condition: Hemorrhoids (Arsh)
- Vata Type: Hard, painful, dry.
- Pitta Type: Bleeding, burning, inflamed.
- Remedy: Use Triphala Guggulu and apply Jatyadi Oil locally. Drink buttermilk (Takra) daily.

# Condition: Hyperacidity Deep Dive
- Focus: Cooling the "Agni" without extinguishing it.
- Support: Avipattikar Churna with coconut water or cold milk.
